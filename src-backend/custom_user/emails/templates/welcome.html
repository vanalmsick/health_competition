Thanks for signing up to the Health Competition!

How does it work?

  • Join a friend's competition using their invitation code or create your own competition.
  • Download the Strava app, connect it to your Apple / Android / Garmin / Fitbit smartwatch, and link your Strava account to the Health Competition. Every morning at 4 am your workouts are automatically imported. If you do not want to link Strava you can also manually log your workouts.
  • Before the competition starts, create or join a team.

How do you earn activity points?

  • Each competition has their own individual activity goals.
  • Each 1% towards an Activity Goal earns you 1 point.
  • E.g. if the workout goal is 100 minutes, working out 50 minutes earns you 50 points.
  • However, there can be upper and lower limits above/below which you don't earn any points (activities that were capped/floored are indicated with an *asterix).
  • Hover with the mouse above a goal to see its limits or above the workout's asterix for more details.
